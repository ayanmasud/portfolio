Oregon Game Project Challenge 2024 — Perpetual Motion

11/4/23 - 5/18/24
Perpetual Motion was created with Brady Rozendaal, Anish Rao, and Collin Wittmayer. It is an artistic puzzle game set in a toy shop during the early 1900s. The shop owner is always hard at work, deciding which toy parts to order and meticulously assembling them to craft new toys for their customers. Experience the relentless pressure of meeting the bare minimum in a capitalistic society. How many weeks can you go?
