Low-Cost Personalized Drowning Detector with Ultrasound Trilateration for Accurate Localization

Sep 2024 - Apr 2025
I created an affordable device that is personally optimized to detect drowning based off your body measurements and depths underwater. After detecting drowning, I utilized ultrasonic transceivers underwater to meticulously perform trilateration to determine the exact position of the drowning swimmer, which enables rapid rescue responses.

I gained experience using electronic circuits such as ultrasonic transceivers, pressure sensors, Arduinos, and buzzers.
